🌟 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get the best skin analysis result?
Use a clear, front-facing photo in good natural light. Avoid harsh shadows, heavy filters, or sunglasses. Make sure your face fills most of the frame.
Why did I get different results for two similar photos?
Lighting, angle, and recent makeup can affect the analysis. For the most consistent reading, use the same photo or capture in similar conditions.
Is my photo stored or shared?
Your photo is stored privately in your account so you can review past analyses. It is never displayed publicly, shared, sold, or used to train any model. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile → Delete Account in the app, or email us at makapp.support@gmail.com. We'll erase all your data within 7 days.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Currently, password reset is in-app via the "Change Password" option in your profile. If you've lost access entirely, email us at makapp.support@gmail.com and we'll help you regain access.
Why is the first scan slow?
The first analysis after install can take 20–30 seconds because our styling engine warms up. Subsequent scans on the same day are much faster.
Are the makeup/skincare recommendations safe to try?
All recommendations are for informational and cosmetic purposes only. They are NOT medical advice. Always patch-test new products and consult a dermatologist if you have skin conditions.
Can I use MAK on iPad?
Yes! MAK works on both iPhone and iPad.
