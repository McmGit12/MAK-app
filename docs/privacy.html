Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

MAK — Your Personalized Skincare, Beauty and Travel Styling Buddy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account in MAK, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • An optional display name
  • A securely-hashed password (using bcrypt — even we cannot read it)

We do NOT collect: your phone number, real name, address, age, location, government IDs, or any device identifiers used for cross-app tracking.

2. Your Photos

Photos you take or upload are sent to our servers for the sole purpose of generating your skincare, makeup, or travel styling recommendations. We do not sell, share, or use your photos to train any model.

Recommendations and resulting analyses are saved to your account so you can revisit them in your History. Your source photo is also stored securely in your account and is never displayed to anyone else.

3. How Your Data Is Protected

  • All app-to-server traffic uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.
  • Your data is stored in an access-controlled database.
  • We never sell, rent, or trade your data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a third-party styling engine to analyze your photos and generate beauty recommendations. The service processes your image solely to return the recommendation result and does not retain it for training. We do not integrate any analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or social trackers.

5. Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account anytime from Profile → Delete Account within the app, or by emailing makapp.support@gmail.com. Account deletion permanently removes your email, password hash, display name, and all past analyses from our servers within 7 days.

6. Children

MAK is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, please contact us and we will delete the account.

7. Disclaimer

MAK provides personalized styling guidance for cosmetic and informational purposes only. Skincare and makeup suggestions are not medical advice and are not a substitute for consultation with a licensed dermatologist or healthcare professional. Try suggested products at your own discretion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email. Continued use of MAK after a policy update means you accept the updated terms.

9. Contact Us

Questions or concerns about your data?

Email: makapp.support@gmail.com

We respond within 5 business days.
