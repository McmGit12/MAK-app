Privacy Policy · Effective Date: June 2025

A friendly note from the MAK team

Hi there 👋 — Thank you for trusting MAK with your beauty journey. Your privacy matters to us as much as your glow. This policy explains, in plain English, what information we handle, why we handle it, and the choices you have. No legal jargon, no confusing clauses — just a straightforward promise to keep your data safe and respect your space.

💖 In short: We collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and you can delete your account whenever you like.

1. What We Collect

To help MAK work smoothly for you, we collect a small amount of information:

  • Account info: Your email address (stored in a hashed, non-reversible form) and a display name you choose. That's it — no phone number, no address, no ID.
  • Analysis preferences: The options you pick in the app (e.g., skin care mode, travel destination, occasion) and the recommendations you receive — so you can revisit them in History.
  • Photos you share: Selfies you upload are processed to give you tailored recommendations and are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.
  • App install info: A random, anonymous device ID and platform (Android/iOS) so we can understand how many people use MAK. This cannot be linked back to you.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • ❌ No personally identifiable information (PII) beyond your email and display name
  • ❌ No payment information (MAK is free to use)
  • ❌ No contact lists, location data, or SMS
  • ❌ No tracking you across other apps or websites

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To sign you in and keep your session secure for 48 hours
  • To save your analysis history so you can revisit past recommendations
  • To personalize suggestions based on preferences you share (skin type, destination, occasion)
  • To improve MAK and fix bugs (using anonymous install counts only)

4. Who We Share Your Data With

Nobody. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for marketing purposes. MAK uses reputable service providers (secure cloud hosting and a managed database) only to store and deliver what you see in the app — and these providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

5. How We Keep Your Data Safe

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption
  • All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted over HTTPS
  • Sessions expire automatically after 48 hours of inactivity
  • Database access is strictly controlled with pooled, authenticated connections

6. Your Choices

  • Update your info: Change your display name or password anytime from Account Settings
  • Delete your account: Email us at the address below and we'll wipe your account within 7 days
  • Opt out of photo analysis: Simply don't upload a photo — skip directly to chat or curated tips
  • Uninstall: Removing the app from your device stops all data collection immediately

7. Children's Privacy

MAK is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us and we will remove the account.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll post the new version here with an updated effective date. For major changes, we'll also notify you inside the app the next time you log in — so you're never caught off guard.
